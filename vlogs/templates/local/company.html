Overview of Bangladesh's Footwear and Leather Industry
Bangladesh's footwear and leather industry is a dynamic sector encompassing tanning, footwear production, leather accessories, and fabric-based footwear. Recognized for quality and competitive pricing, the country supplies major international brands like Aldo, Timberland, and H&M.
Export Markets
The primary export destinations include the European Union, United States, Japan, the UK, and China, highlighting the global acceptance of Bangladeshi products.
Global Market Potential/h6>
Valued at USD 394.12 billion in 2020, the global leather goods market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.9% through 2028. Increasing disposable incomes and evolving fashion trends drive demand for stylish leather products, presenting significant opportunities for Bangladeshi companies to expand their international reach.
